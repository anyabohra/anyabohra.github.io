Privacy Policy [Updated: 6/4/24]

Information We Collect

User Profile Data:

  • When you sign up for an account, we collect your name, email address, age, and skill level.
  • This information is used to create a private profile accessible only to you.

Tournament Tracking:

  • We collect data related to the tournaments you track, including tournament names, dates, and brackets.
  • This helps us provide personalized tournament updates and live bracket information.

How We Use Your Data

  • [Personalization] We use your profile data to personalize your experience within the app. For example, we may recommend tournaments based on your skill level.
  • [Communication] We may send you notifications or emails about upcoming tournaments or changes to tournament schedules.
  • [Data Security] Your personal information is securely stored and accessible only to you. We do not share your data with third parties.

Data Retention

  • We retain your profile data as long as you have an active account.
  • If you delete your account, your data will be permanently removed from our servers.

Your Rights

  • You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information.
  • Contact our support team if you need assistance with any of these actions.

Changes to this Policy

  • We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Check our app or website for the latest version.
